    PREPARATION

STEPS

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) for 10 to 15 minutes.

Combine the rice flour, oil, asafoetida and salt in a bowl and mix well. Add the curds and a little water and knead into soft dough. Put the mixture into a chakli press and press out round whirls of the dough onto a greased baking tray, working closely from the centre to the outside to the whirl. Bake these chaklis.
